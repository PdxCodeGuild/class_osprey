Danny DeVito

Danny Devito is an American actor, comedian, and filmmaker. He has won an Emmy and Golden Globe Award and is known for his height. Although he has starred in many classic movies such as Matilda and Batman Returns, his role in It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia has become a pop culture icon.

“We don't even know if we're in this room. We could be in a turtle's dream in outer space.” — Danny DeVito
